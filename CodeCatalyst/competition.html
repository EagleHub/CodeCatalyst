HOSA Competitions

In the upcoming future, we have a few competitions in which our students work to show the skills that they have acquired from their teachers and the ways in which they can use them. As our students go through their recommended medial courses some find that they are more interested in a certain field and so as they work to learn more about it, they are given the opportunity to show what they have learned in the form of a competition.
