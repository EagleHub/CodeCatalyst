Chester County High School's HOSA

Welcome to CCHS's HOSA club!
HOSA-Future Health Professionals is a national student organization advocated by the U.S. Department of Science Education Division of ACTE. We have a mission to promote career opportunities in health care and to improve the quality of healthcare provided to all people. HOSA members are provided with a unique program of leadership development, motivation, and recognition exclusively for secondary, post-secondary, adult, and collegiate students enrolled in the health science programs.

Chester County High School HOSA club now has 134 members. The main mission of our club is to work on the shortage of qualified workers for the healthcare industry, and to encourage our students to work hard for their goals in the healthcare profession.
